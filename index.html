Game setup

Pick a scenario, then pass the device.

One person sets up the game. Then each of the 7 players takes a private turn — picks a role, reads a briefing, and makes their case. The President goes last, reads all the arguments, and delivers the final decision.

How it works
1. Choose a crisis scenario below.
2. Pass the device around — each player picks their role and builds their argument privately.
3. After all 7 advisors go, the President reads every argument and makes the final call.
4. Everyone gets scored and receives feedback.
