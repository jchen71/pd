Game setup

Pick a scenario, then pass the device.

One person sets up the game. Then each of the 7 players takes a private turn — picks a role, reads a briefing, and makes their case. When everyone's done, the President reads all the arguments and decides.

How it works
1. Choose a crisis scenario below.
2. Pass the device around — each player picks their role and builds their argument privately.
3. After all 7 players go, the full debate plays out and the President delivers a verdict.
4. Everyone gets scored and receives feedback.
